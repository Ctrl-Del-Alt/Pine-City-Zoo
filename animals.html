ANIMALS MAP PLACES

ELEPHANTS

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth. They are known for their intelligence, social behavior, and strong family bonds.

GEMSBOKS

Gemsbok are large antelopes that are native to the arid regions of southern Africa. They are known for their long, straight horns and striking appearance.

GIRAFFES

Giraffes are the tallest land animals, known for their long necks and legs. They are native to Africa and are often found in savannas and open woodlands.

PANDAS

Pandas are large bears native to China, known for their distinctive black and white fur. They primarily eat bamboo and are considered a symbol of conservation efforts.

MONKEYS

Monkeys are primates known for their intelligence and social behavior. They are found in various habitats around the world, from rainforests to savannas.

GORILLAS
